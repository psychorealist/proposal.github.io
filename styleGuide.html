Style Guide
Content//design//interaction
The website will feature a Narrative Html structure which will focus on story telling in a creative fashion. The overall design will lean heavily on a glitchy aesthetic with lines of animated error code and the story text. The backdrop will feature animated glitch visuals with high contrasting colors for the overall color scheme. The interactions will mostly be scroll based with some added interactivity into the story that will let the user have some limited influence on the story
